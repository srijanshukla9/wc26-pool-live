Scoring Guide

How scoring works (the official pool rules, max 470 pts) — Groups: 3 pts per correct advancing team · +4 per exact group position · 3 pts per correct third-place group. Knockouts: Round-of-32 winners 3 · Round-of-16 winners 4 · Quarterfinal winners 5 · Runner-up 8 · Champion 50.
Points is one live number: we take the current group tables as if they were final and apply the rules above, recomputed after every game — so it moves with every result. Secured is the floor that can never be lost; Ceiling is the highest you can still finish.
Win% comes from Monte Carlo simulation of the remaining tournament using Elo ratings.
