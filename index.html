Scoring Guide

How scoring works (mirrors the official pool, max 470 pts) — Groups: 3 pts per correct advancing team · +4 per exact group position · 3 pts per correct third-place group. Knockouts: Round-of-32 winners 3 · Round-of-16 winners 4 · Quarterfinal winners 5 · Runner-up 8 · Champion 50.
Projected scores groups as if today's live tables were final, so it moves with every goal. Official follows the pool site's rule — group points settle once all groups finish — and stays the canonical score for settling.
Matchday Crown 👑 — most points scored inside each window: R1 (11–18 Jun), R2 (19–24 Jun), R3 (25–28 Jun), then R32, R16, QF, SF and the Finals. Win% comes from Monte Carlo simulation of the remaining tournament using Elo ratings.
